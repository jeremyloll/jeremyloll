## Hi, I'm Jeremy 👋

Data analyst and web developer based in Kerala, India. I've spent 5+ years working with data — including several years as a content writer and analyst in the Indian football circuit. I built [TACTIX](tactix-by-jeremy.streamlit.app) out of a personal frustration: I wanted instant visualisations from ISL data and couldn't find anything that worked for me, so I built it myself. Later built a government tracker to track the promises in UDF Manifesto being implemented in Kerala [Keralam Government Tracker](https://keralam-govt-tracker.pages.dev/)

Currently open to **remote data analyst** and **freelance analytics/visualisation** work.

---

### 🔨 What I'm building

**TACTIX** — a Streamlit-based football analytics platform for the Indian Super League. Current sprint: shots conceded heatmaps with KDE density overlay, layered scatter plots, and StatsBomb coordinate conventions.
**Keralam Government** — Tracking the promises the current ruling party promised in their manifesto. Planning to extend it to more governments including previous state governments and central government.
